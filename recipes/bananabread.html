Banana Bread

Nutrition Facts :
Per serving : 229 calories; protein 3.8g; carbohydrates 34.8g; fat 8.8g; cholesterol 51.3mg; sodium 222.9mg.

  • Cook: 1 hr 5 mins
  • Total: 1 hr 20 mins
  • Prep: 15 mins
  • Servings: 12
  • Yield: 1 loaf

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • ½ cup butter
  • ¾ cup brown sugar
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 2⅓ cups mashed overripe bananas

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Lightly grease a 9x5 inch loaf pan.
  2. In a large bowl, combine flour, baking soda and salt. In a separate bowl, cream together butter and brown sugar.
    Stir in eggs and mashed bananas until well blended. Stir banana mixture into flour mixture; stir just to moisten.
    Pour batter into prepared loaf pan.
  3. Bake in preheated oven for 60 to 65 minutes, until a toothpick inserted into center of the loaf comes out clean.
    Let bread cool in pan for 10 minutes, then turn out onto a wire rack.
